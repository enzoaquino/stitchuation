Frequently Asked Questions

How do I delete my account?

Send an email to support@stitchuation.app with the subject "Account Deletion" and we will delete your account and all associated data within 30 days.

How do I manage my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through the Apple App Store. Open Settings on your device, tap your name, then tap Subscriptions to view, change, or cancel your Stitchuation subscription.

How do I request a copy of my data?

Email privacy@stitchuation.app with the subject "Data Export Request" and we will send you a copy of your data.

I forgot my password

If you signed up with email and password, you can reset it from the login screen. If you signed in with Apple, Facebook, or TikTok, use that same provider to sign in again — no password is needed.
